How Our System Works

Our Kidney Disease Detection and Prediction System uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze your medical parameters and predict the likelihood of kidney disease. Early detection can significantly improve treatment outcomes and quality of life.

  • Fill out all required medical parameters in the test form
